About Me

Passionate Data Scientist and Engineer with a focus on solving real-world problems through innovative technology.

Hi, I'm Omar Ouchchen, a first-year engineering student at the École de Science de l'Information, with a specialization in data science. I am passionate about using data-driven insights and cutting-edge technologies to solve complex challenges. Over the years, I've honed my skills in various areas, including data analysis, machine learning, and software development.

In addition to my academic journey, I have had the opportunity to work on projects related to document management systems, where I applied my expertise in OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and Python programming. During my internship at Doosys, I worked on developing an Odoo-based application to streamline document archiving and processing for banks in Morocco. This experience strengthened my ability to work with ERP systems and enhanced my understanding of real-world business applications.

I am always eager to learn and grow, and I look forward to future opportunities where I can apply my skills in data science and engineering to drive innovation and contribute to impactful projects.

If you're interested in collaborating or discussing potential opportunities, feel free to reach out to me via the contact section or directly through my LinkedIn or GitHub profiles.
